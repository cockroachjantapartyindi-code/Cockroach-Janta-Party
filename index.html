The Ground Reality

What We Are Fighting For.

We are done with manufactured outrages. The CJP stands for urgent action on the everyday disasters affecting the common citizen.

Issue / 01📉

Unemployment & Underemployment

A rapidly growing workforce with degrees but no opportunities or fair wages. Millions of qualified Indians are jobless or trapped in work far below their potential.

Issue / 02📝

Paper Leaks & Exam Corruption

Syndicates are destroying the futures of millions of honest students. Demand harsh, immediate penalties for every person in the chain — top to bottom.

Issue / 03🛒

Cost of Living & Inflation (Mehngai)

Basic survival — food, housing, fuel — has become unaffordable. Policies that make the rich richer while squeezing everyone else must be challenged.

Issue / 04🏛️

Corruption & Bureaucracy

Red tape and bribery are required to get basic public work done. Dismantling this systemic rot is the foundation of every other reform.

Issue / 05🏗️

Weak Public Infrastructure

Where does our tax money go when bridges collapse and roads wash away? We demand a full public audit — every rupee accounted for.

Issue / 06🏥

Education & Healthcare

Crumbling public schools and overburdened government hospitals that fail the poorest citizens. An overhaul is a constitutional obligation.

Issue / 07🌾

Farmer Distress

Fair pricing, debt relief, and sustainable agricultural policies for the backbone of our country. The people who feed India should never die in debt.

Issue / 08🌫️

Pollution & Environmental Damage

Toxic air and polluted water are not inconveniences — they are national emergencies killing people every day. Treat them as such, immediately.

Issue / 09⚖️

Judicial Delays

Justice delayed for decades is justice denied. Faster courts, more judges, digitized case management — solutions exist. Political will is missing.

Issue / 10🔥

Political Polarization & Division

Communal divides are manufactured tools of distraction. We reject every attempt to pit Indian against Indian while real issues go unaddressed.
