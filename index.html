The 5 Demands

The Manifesto.

Simple. Non-negotiable. Written by people who actually read the news instead of just appearing on it.

01
Judiciary

No Rajya Sabha seat for retired Chief Justices.

If you protected the Constitution for 30 years, you don't get a reward seat in Parliament six months later. Pick a lane.

02
Elections

UAPA arrests for Election Commissioners who delete legit votes.

Misuse of power cuts both ways. If UAPA can silence dissent, it can silence election fraud. Simple math.

03
Representation

50% reservation for women in Parliament & Cabinet.

Half the population. Half the seats. Not revolutionary — just arithmetic. We'll wait.

04
Media

Cancel media licenses for oligarchs; investigate Godi media anchors.

A free press is not a press that's been bought. Investigate every prime-time anchor who traded journalism for access.

05
Accountability

20-year election ban for defecting MLAs/MPs.

You were elected on a mandate. Jumping ship mid-term is betrayal. We'll keep the scoreboard.
