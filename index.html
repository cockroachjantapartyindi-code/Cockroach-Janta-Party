The Movement's Vision

A razor-sharp political platform for India's disillusioned youth. We are politically aware, deeply impacted by systemic failures, and tired of being gaslit by empty promises, endless election cycles, and noisy primetime panels. It is time to demand absolute accountability for the real crises crippling our nation's future.

Who We Represent

We represent the millions inheriting a broken economy. The students whose futures are sold to the highest bidder in paper leaks. The hardworking citizens crushed by daily inflation and stagnant wages. We are the voices drowned out by corruption and propaganda. We rant professionally — and protest fiercely — because the system has stopped listening.

The Ground Reality

What We Are Fighting For.

We are done with manufactured outrages. The CJP stands for urgent action on the everyday disasters affecting the common citizen.

📉

Unemployment & Underemployment

A rapidly growing workforce with degrees but no opportunities or fair wages. Millions of qualified Indians are jobless or trapped in work far below their potential.

📝

Paper Leaks & Exam Corruption

Syndicates are destroying the futures of millions of honest students. Demand harsh, immediate penalties for every person in the chain — top to bottom.

🛒

Cost of Living & Inflation (Mehngai)

Basic survival — food, housing, fuel — has become unaffordable. Policies that make the rich richer while squeezing everyone else must be challenged.

🏛️

Corruption & Bureaucracy

Red tape and bribery are required to get basic public work done. Dismantling this systemic rot is the foundation of every other reform.

🏗️

Weak Public Infrastructure

Where does our tax money go when bridges collapse, roads wash away, and public transit remains unsafe? We demand a full public audit — every rupee.

🏥

Education & Healthcare

Crumbling public schools and overburdened government hospitals that fail the poorest citizens. An overhaul is a constitutional obligation, not a demand.

🌾

Farmer Distress

Fair pricing, debt relief, and sustainable agricultural policies for the backbone of our country. The people who feed India should never go hungry or die in debt.

🌫️

Pollution & Environmental Damage

Toxic air and polluted water are not inconveniences — they are national emergencies killing people every day. Treat them as such, immediately.

⚖️

Judicial Delays

Justice delayed for decades is justice denied. Faster courts, more judges, digitized case management — the solutions exist. Political will is what's missing.

🔥

Political Polarization & Social Division

Communal divides are manufactured tools of distraction. We reject every attempt to pit Indian against Indian while real issues go unaddressed.
